About Me

Hi, I'm Awoyemi Abiola, A 21 Year old Front-end Developer, with two years experience building aesthetic and user-friendly interfaces by utilizing web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript, as well as web development tools and libraries like Git, SASS/SCSS and GSAP.

My Core Strengths as a Front-end Developer are Dedication to my work, Creativity and a near-obsessive attention to detail.

I Currently aim to achieve highest level attainable as a Front-end developer, getting a Practical Knowledge of API's and picking up useful Javascript Frameworks are the next steps for me in this Journey.

When I'm not developing websites and Applications, I'm Either talking to friends, playing Games, or attempting to break the world record for the fastest time to solve a rubik's cube.

Bringing Ideas To Life

Of all my core strengths, I Consider Creativity to be my Strongest Suit. There is a popular saying that gets passed around every now and then in the tech space, that "Developers Can't Design", which is supposed to perpetuate the notion that Front-end Developers are incapable of creating beatiful interfaces without some sort of reference.

This has always appeared alien to me, Since the only "reference" i have ever had to work with for all of my personal projects, is an image of what i want in my head, an Idea, if you will, which i then build upon till i have been able to bring this Idea to life. Work with me and let's bring all your ideas to life.
